Aside from my various hobbies, I love to spend time working on my academics, with my family, playing cello in one of my orchestras, and working in my community!

ABOUT ME

Since I was a kid, I was always interested in video games and computers. Soon, I developed a desire to start making, instead of playing them, and learnt more about coding (c#, java, and html to be specific). Now, I am using my past skills to make new projects that combine my newfound interest in physics with my knowledge of computer science.
Cello is another passion of mine, in which I started when I was five. Over the last 10 (almost eleven!) years I learnt many new and interesting songs and techniques, and even dabbled in arranging music! I am part of both the Contra Costa Chamber Orchestra, the Center For Community Arts Cello Ensemble, which is a group of cello players that are all in high school and preform at various concerts and events, and I also am the first chair cellist in my school's orchestra. Other groups I am a part of are the The Young People's Symphony Orchestra, and the East Bay Chamber Orchestra.

My cello teacher is Stephen Evens, who has a BA in Cello Preformance and a MA in Orchestra Performance. He leads my Cello Ensemble, and is an incredibly skilled and passionate teacher. He can be contacted at stephen.evans29@gmail.com.
