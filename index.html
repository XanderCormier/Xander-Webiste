LESSONS

SPECIFICS

I do a 30 minute or longer lessons for beginner cellists aged around 5-12. It is preferred that they have little to no experience.
For a 30 minute lesson, I charge about $30.
My lessons are based in Walnut Creek, so it would be best if you lived in or near Contra-Costa County.

NECESSARY MATERIALS

The materials needed for learining the cello are as followed:

  1. A Rented Cello
  2. A Rented Bow
  3. Suzuki Cello School, Vol. 1

There are several music stores around the bay area that offer renting services, repairs, and music.
Here are some of the ones that have great reputations:

  • Lamorinda Music
  • Ifshin Violins

In the music stores, you should ask if they can find a rental cello and bow that fits your child, as it is very hard to learn an instrument that is the wrong size.
For the music book, you could either order it off of Amazon, or find it in one of the music stores above.
